FOOD NOSTALGIA

Nostalgia is a sentiment or emotion that is inevitable for anyone as it comes out naturally. Human beings gain memories every now and then, researches show that our sense of smell and taste is strongly associated to our memory.

Memories and food collectively constitutes nostalgia. Food memories take us back to our old sweet past events. It make us reminisce our loved ones and sometimes even strangers too. When we recall memories related to food items like Paranthe. pastas or anything tasteful that is tempting to our minds, hearts, tongues and souls. Hostellers or people away from home usually face these emotions. Home food is also a type of emotion involved. Maggie, a very common close to heart food item but connected with a lot of a lot of people like mother who makes it super delicious one friends and of a specific place which evoke memories.

Happiness, Comfort and Taste makes it all nostalgic. Photographs helps us revive those emotions again. Especially in current situations like COVID 19, these moments are worth remembering it.

Brought up in a Punjabi family, for me CHURI is a food item cooked and served by my granny that burst me out into emotions and well spent moments with my grandmother. Each person has a different sense of food nostalgia related to them especially traditional food that keeps us inter connected to our folklore, rituals and beliefs.
