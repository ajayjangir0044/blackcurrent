COLLEGE LIFE

College life has given me the confidence to fail, in order to succeed in life. College life- a paradise for students where they get freedom and experiences altogether. Commencing in school, ending at college is a whole new journey. It exposes us to unfamiliar people and things. For some students, it is an adventure and for some it is just simply great. One might see this moment to live the life at fullest and other see it as an opportunity to follow their passions by working hard. Everyone wants to go through this beautiful journey in their lives.

Beautiful journey start with exploring the college and one throughout factor there for us are friends and groups that we came across by some common friends or just randomly and ultimately they become a major part of our common life, This expedition starts from an immature student coming to college with dreams in their eyes and ends up with tones of memories in his life. Different moral and mental qualities are followed up than what you were actually doing till now.

College life is much interesting than any other thing because we crawl at a pace where we are enjoying every day participating in clubs, doing parties, competing student elections, part time jobs ,and doing everything aside academics. College life is a stage which helps indivuals to understand the reality in a better way.

On the other hand is Academics, which is a common goal of college students to aspire for their goals and find what they actually want from their lives. It provides a panorama of fun, enjoyment, and relationships with teachers, classmates or other staff working there. So, we develop as a person, our knowledge and responsibilities increases too.
