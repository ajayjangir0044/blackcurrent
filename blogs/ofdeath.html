OF DEATHS

Death and ghosts have one thing in common, people are curious about them and often discussion of one leads to another, real bad deaths are not one which is gruesome but those which have a sense of fun element associated with them, like an ox coming from behind shove his horn in your buttocks and throw you away causing death, though your nearest kin may feel only one emotion and that is of sorrow, same can't be said about others, and if you have done mediocre sins so that you are denied entry into heaven and also rejected by hell and left to wander like a ghost you can see this bizarre funny-sad moment where whom you considered all your life as Nobel soul maybe will be whispering about your death to someone else while having an unexplainable release of endorphins from their body, and the funny part is maybe you do not feel that bad about them laughing at your death standing just behind your graveyard, well that's only possible if one believe in existence of ghosts like that of a society where communism is accepted with heart and mind.

If death and birth are considered to be a cycle, and you are having a hard time dealing with your present life then why not to die, who knows you take birth to the royal family of Britain or end up being a son of an owner of a strip club, though the probability of both cases is pretty low and depends on the family planning of respective responsible people then again you have 'n' chances which are quite alot to adopt trial and error method if the sky is the limit for 'n', if one goes to hell or heaven depending on one's deeds and the number of times it takes gods name, and it is assummed that death and sins both can be quantified then why not to die sooner, it will decrease the possibility of one doing sins or you can calculate your sins and take god name that many times before your predecided doomsday so as to certain your ticket to heaven with a condition that you do not procastinate, maybe you can avail early reaching benefit if heaven has the policy of first cum first serve one of them being you can have more then 72 hoors for you.

For a person from neutral reference frame death is a normal and often happening event, one who says dead people should not be inculpated or criticized are either ignorant or foolish or hypocrites because history judges everything, even nature and way of death and historians who are hell-bound committed to some ideology are like maulvis in a devotee Muslim society who are Arabic illiterate, sometimes the righteousness and truthfulness of event do not matter, what matter is acceptance of certain individuals as intellectuals by the society who can create another perception of death which suits them and their interests.

sometimes death can be a good way to become famous especially when you have carved for it in your whole life and the type of public recognition is immaterial to you, you can die while taking selfie on the banks of a flooding river or while you are experiencing ultimate societal taboo bliss and your heart fail due to excess of sildenafil in your body.

Death can be profitable too, but often one has to pay a high price for it

sometimes death seems to be the best way to keep your dignity especially when your recent actions have not been as per your expectations or according to principles of your life , it might feel like the easiest way to get out of the situation only if almighty decide not to give you a lifeline and you end up in a hospital.

Death will start looking more alluring if scientists somehow find a way to bring a dead Homosapien back as it will be the best way to become slim while burning fat through an anaerobic process.

Fools fear death, wise men accept it as universal truth and mystics plan their schedule according to it

Fear of death is often the best business model to earn money.

At the moment the writer was writing this piece, death was still being considered inevitable, and dying for one's country and dignity as the best means to accept it, the writer may or may not agree with it.
